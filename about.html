WebWellness

About

Our mission is to empower individuals regarding their health and seek health information online in a way that does not result in a bottomless pit of fear. Online searches for answers may yield either comfortable or alarming information, often the latter. Our goal is to help people find themselves trapped in a cycle of worry, rationalize their fears, and effectively navigate the wealth of health information online.
