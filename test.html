E-Health Literacy Quiz

1. Where should you check for credible health information online?

✓ Official health organization websites

2. What is important to review when visiting a health website?

✓ Privacy policy

3. Which of the following is a sign of biased health information?

✓ Information promoting a specific product for profit

4. How often should you verify the currency of health information online?

✓ Every time you consult it

5. What is a key component of e-health literacy?

✓ Ability to evaluate the reliability of online health information

6. How can you communicate securely with your healthcare provider online?

✓ Through a secure patient portal provided by the healthcare provider

7. What should you consider when choosing a health app?

✓ Privacy policy and data security measures

8. Which feature indicates that a health information website is secure?

✓ URL starts with HTTPS
