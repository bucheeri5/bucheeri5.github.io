What are Health Records?

Verify the Source

Always check the credibility of the information source. Look for information from reputable healthcare institutions, government health departments, or accredited health professionals.

Understand Privacy

Be aware of the privacy policies of the health websites you visit. Understand how your data is collected, used, and protected.

Seek Professional Advice

Use online health information as a supplement, not a substitute for professional healthcare advice. Always consult healthcare professionals for health concerns.

Enhance Digital Skills

Improve your computer and internet skills. Familiarity with digital tools and resources can significantly enhance your ability to access and evaluate health information online.
