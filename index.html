Webwellness: maintaining health and wellbeing through the web for Glasgow University students

MAD(A) Project Homepage

Welcome to my page! The primary focus of this portfolio is to explore how the web can be leveraged to improve access to mental health resources, particularly for students at Glasgow University. It aims to:

    Incorporate interactive elements and use multimedia to promote wellbeing strategies
    Addresss common myths and misconceptions about mental health, especially within the digital age
    Emphasize the importance of stress management and maintaining good mental health, offering guidance on achieving these goals
    Signpost Glasgow University students to available internal and external resources
